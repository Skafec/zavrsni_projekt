My story

My story starts at 2nd year of collage. Before that I had no particular ambitions or any vision of what interested me. Then I found out about HTML and CSS. After finishing class I decided that I want to develop web sites. From then I tryed to learn as much as I could about developing web pages.

I met photography at my second year of collage as well. I always liked arts, so entering world of photography was an amazing experience. From that point forward I wanted to and still want to learn as much as I could about photography.

I liked 3D modeling as well, so enrolling video animation class was a smart choice. There I had opportunity to learn about 3D modeling, texturing. lights, and animation.

I plan on finishing my collage education around end of 2018. I hope to find a job doing web design and/or development before I graduate collage.
